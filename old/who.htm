Simon Cummin: Production Director

Simon is Production Director on the board of directors of Feed The Ducks Productions Limited. He became involved with the company after meeting Newton and Merry while acting as Technical Director of Jazzmatazz, C-1, C-Venues 2004. He then stuck with them for the following two productions, Technical Directing Apocalypse: The Musical in Cambridge and the Fringe and Directing HappyLand at Goldsmith's. Outside of his Feed The Ducks experience, Simon is employed by Royal Holloway College, University of London where he is a Lighting Assistant in the Drama Department, where he is also completing a degree in Drama and Theatre Studies. He has just finished Technical Directing Sweeney Todd in the Jane Holloway Hall, Royal Holloway and Max and Iván: Exposed at the Etcetera Theatre, Camden. This year he has also Technical Directed Closer by Patrick Marber and Five Women Wearing the Same Dress by Alan Ball. He has been a part of Feed the Ducks Productions Ltd growing from a group of friends to a small but perfectly formed production company and is thrilled to be a part of it.



Heather Newton: Creative Director


As one half of the writing partnership of Newton & Merry, Heather's heart lies in the creative side of Musical Theatre, befitting her role as Creative Director of Feed The Ducks Productions Limited. She has just completed an MA in Writing for Musical Theatre, with distinction, and is keen to get back to the Fringe. Previous experience during her time at Cambridge includes Directorial roles of Apocalypse: The Musical, Salad Days, and A Servant To Two Masters. Beyond the creative, Heather has had varied experience of the production of shows whilst acting as the President of a busy Cambridge drama society which funded and supported around 20 shows and events a year. She acted as the main Producer of Apocalypse: The Musical for its Edinburgh run at C Venues in 2005, and would love to see it on stage again- hopefully this time a little earlier than 11.30pm!



Dom Carter: Musical Director

Dom is a man of many talents: musical director, composer, arranger, orchestrator, pianist, organist, singer, teacher, voice coach. To let you in on a little secret, Dom is the man behind the pseudonym "Ernest Merry", composer of Jazzmatazz, Apocalypse: The Musical and HappyLand, the latter of which is currently receiving its finishing touches. Dom is a graduate of Pembroke College, Cambridge, where he was Organ Scholar and read Music. Following this he trained in Musical Theatre at the Royal Academy of Music, and he has studied accompanying with Simon Smith and Alexis French. Dom's MD credits across Cambridge and London include Guys and Dolls, Salad Days, The Best Little Whorehouse in Texas, The Young Ones, Pirates of Penzance, A Tale of Two Cities, Dracula and national tours of All The World's A Stage and Cool Britannia! He has recently finished working as MD for a recording of The Rose Tattoo (Pickering/Loveless) and is currently writing/arranging his 2nd album.
